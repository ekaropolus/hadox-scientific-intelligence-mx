# Data and third-party terms

This repository contains source code, documentation, schemas, and templates. It does **not** include the operational datasets, private crosswalks, client records, report bundles, or named outreach lists used by earlier private prototypes.

The Apache-2.0 license applies only to Hadox-authored material in this repository. It does not grant rights to data obtained from INEGI, SNII/SECIHTI, OpenAlex, ROR, government portals, commercial registries, or any other external source.

Before adding a fixture, cached response, extract, or generated table:

1. record the source URL, owner, retrieval date, version, and applicable terms;
2. confirm that redistribution and the proposed use are permitted;
3. remove or aggregate personal and sensitive fields unless publication has a documented lawful basis;
4. prefer synthetic fixtures when redistribution rights are unclear; and
5. keep credentials, licensed raw files, and private entity-resolution tables outside Git.

Links and source names in the documentation are citations and workflow references, not copies of those sources and not a representation that Hadox can relicense them.
