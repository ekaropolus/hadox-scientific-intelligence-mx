# Community code of conduct

We want this project to be a rigorous, welcoming place for researchers, developers, public-interest practitioners, and domain experts.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss evidence, code, and decisions without attacking people.
- Explain uncertainty, assumptions, conflicts of interest, and source limitations.
- Respect privacy, consent, attribution, and the terms of external sources.
- Welcome corrections and make review accessible to people with different backgrounds.
- Use synthetic or approved redistributable data in issues, examples, and pull requests.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, publication of private data, credential disclosure, deliberate source misrepresentation, and attempts to bypass a source's access or licensing controls are not accepted.

## Reporting

For community concerns, contact `hi@hadox.org`. For vulnerabilities or accidental exposure of sensitive material, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and do not open a public issue.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect contributors, users, or data subjects.
